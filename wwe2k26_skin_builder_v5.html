Apply in WWE 2K▾

1. Upload PNGs

  1. Export skin PNGs from this app (right panel).
  2. Upload via the WWE 2K Image Uploader website using your game-linked account.
  3. In game: Online → Community Creations → Image Manager → Incoming Images → Download.

2. Place each tile

  • Torso Front — over chest and abs.
  • Torso Back — upper and lower back.
  • Left/Right Arm — shoulder to wrist on each arm.
  • Left/Right Leg Front — thigh and shin front.
  • Left/Right Leg Back — hamstring and calf back.
  • Glutes — rear hip area bridging torso and legs.

3. Layering tips

  • Place skin overlays low in the layer stack; logos and gear go above.
  • Slightly overscale tiles so feathered edges overlap at joints.
  • For fitness/bikini characters keep opacity high for contour; reduce slightly if too harsh.
